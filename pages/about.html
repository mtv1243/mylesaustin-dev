About Myles

Myles Austin Tyrer-Vasell is an Ann Arbor-based full-stack web developer working in JavaScript, Node.js, React.js, HTML5, CSS, and any other tools he can get his hands on that help him create.

Before coming to tech, Myles got a degree in film production from Brandeis University in Waltham MA, then moved to NYC and became a bicycle rental agent, cinematographer, steakhouse waiter, documentary filmmaker, and Google Ads guru. After taking an introductory HTML and CSS class at DCTV in Manhattan, Myles began peeking behind the curtain of all his favorite websites so he could start making his own.

He might not have met you yet, but would love to teach you how to play cribbage.

He won't go easy on you.
